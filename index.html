UBC Workday → ICS

Drop your Current_Schedule.xlsx from Workday. Get an .ics file you can import into any calendar app. Everything runs in your browser — your schedule never leaves your computer.

How to get your Workday Excel file
  1. Sign in at myworkday.ubc.ca.
  2. Open Academics, then the Registration & Courses tab.
  3. In the Current Schedule block, click the settings wheel and download the Excel file (Current_Schedule.xlsx).
